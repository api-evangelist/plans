One of the primary objectives for API resources existing is in support of acquiring data and content that fuels platform operations. This is a very common element of successful API platforms that are operating today, such as Crunchbase or Angellist, or even Twitter and Tumblr. These are all examples of platforms where content acquisition is a core element of their API plans.

Organizations

  • Medium - Medium is a pretty good example of an API, where the focus is about content acquisition. Something that may change in future, but for now that is primary focus.
  • Facebook - Facebook is a pretty classic example of a platform that is about content acquisition, and one that is heavily focused on advertising.
  • Twitter - And yet another pretty classic example of a platform that is about content acquisition, and is heavily focused on advertising -- Twitter.
