Is there a trial period that is limited by number of days, which may not apply to all plans. Not all audiences will respond to trial levels of access, but may work will with incentivizing higher levels of access, and API consumption. Refrain from requiring credit cards for trial levels of access, as it deters signup from abuse by other providers.

  • SendGrid - Sendgrid gives you a 45 day trial to see how much volume you will send, and help you determine which tier you should be in.
